Grilled Garlic Butter Corn Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 ears of fresh corn, husked
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped parsley or coriander (optional)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Grill
    Heat your grill to medium-high.
  2. Make Garlic Butter
    In a small bowl, mix melted butter with minced garlic and a pinch of salt.
  3. Grill the Corn
    Place corn directly on the grill. Cook for 8–10 minutes, turning occasionally, until kernels are lightly charred.
  4. Brush & Serve
    Brush the hot corn with garlic butter. Sprinkle with herbs if desired and serve with lemon wedges.
